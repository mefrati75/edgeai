The Computing Pendulum

History doesn't repeat, but it rhymes. Computing swings between centralized and distributed.

🖥️ Mainframe Era

1960s-1970s

It all started as "cloud" - X.25 terminals connected to massive mainframes. Your computer was somewhere else. Sound familiar?

💾 Personal Computer Revolution

1980s-1990s

The pendulum swung: PCs brought computing home. Your data, your device, your control. Zero cloud.

☁️ The Cloud Era

2000s-2020

The pendulum swung back: Everything moved to the cloud. We recreated mainframes, just called them something else.

🚀 Edge Intelligence Era

2024+

The pendulum swings again: AI makes devices smarter than servers. Computing returns home, but this time with superpowers.
